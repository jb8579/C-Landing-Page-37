2.1 Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help improve website functionality, enhance user experience, and enable certain features such as secure login and personalization. 2.2 We use both session cookies (which expire when you close your browser) and persistent cookies (which remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them).

They enable core functions such as account login, security, and transaction processing. 3.2 Performance and Analytics Cookies: These cookies help us analyze how users interact with our website by collecting information such as pages visited, time spent on the site, and error messages. We use this data to improve website performance and user experience. 3.3 Functionality Cookies: These cookies allow us to remember your preferences and settings (such as language and region) to enhance your browsing experience. 3.4 Advertising and Marketing Cookies: These cookies track your online activity to deliver relevant advertisements.
